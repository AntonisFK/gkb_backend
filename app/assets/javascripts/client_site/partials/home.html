Transportation for Transformation

Mission Statement

Good Karma Bikes is a nonprofit social enterprise transforming the lives of former foster and other disadvantaged youth in Silicon Valley by providing the means for secondary education, job training, and life skills though the sale of safe, reliable refurbished bicycles to local community members.
