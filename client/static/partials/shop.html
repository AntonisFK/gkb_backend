How We Get Our Bikes

We receive donated bikes from shops, manufacturers, universities, public offices, and individuals just like you! We refurbish these bikes for resale and granting. For each bicycle sold, one is granted to someone in need.

How We Price

GKB offers prices similar to those you will find online and in other second-hand bike shops, but we offer something they don’t… our low price includes all parts and labor needed to refurbish your bicycle.
